My CV

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison advised by Prof. Paris Koutris. My research interest lies in the intersection of theoretical computer science and databases. Currently, I am working on efficient algorithms to compute the consistent query answers on databases that may violate the primary key constraint.

Before that, I obtained my B.Sc. in computer science from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) in 2018. I was fortunate to work with Prof. Yixin Cao on graph theory and parameterized algorithms. I was an exchange student at the University of Waterloo in Winter 2017.
