My CV

I am a second year graduate student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison broadly interested in theoretical computer science, database systems and optimization theory. I am now working with Professor Paris Koutris on the intersection between theory and databases.

My undergraduate advisor is Professor Yixin Cao, and I am fortunate to work with him on algorithmic graph theory and parameterized algorithms for three years.

I graduated from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) with my bachelor's degree in computer science in 2018, and I was an exchange student at the University of Waterloo in Winter 2017.
